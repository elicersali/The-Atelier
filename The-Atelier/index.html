What we do

Our goal is to create a wardrobe that will be versatile enough to complement each and every occasion.

Wardrobe Planning

Do you have an upcoming special event? We can help with that. Along with curating new outfits, recommendations will be made to enhance and refresh your current wardrobe.

Personal Shopping

Our desire is to help you feel your best ever in the skin you're in. We will taylor items to match your budget, occasion and style.

Closet Consultation

Tired of holding on to clothes you never wear? Allow us to spruce up your closet to get an idea of your personal style. Through this process, we will also declutter items no longer needed and create flatteting outfits that will help you put your best foot forward.

Personal Style analysis

After being paired with one of our amazing stylists, you will go through a series of questions to identify and discuss your comfort and personal style.
